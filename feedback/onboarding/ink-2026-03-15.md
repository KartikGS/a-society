## Reconnaissance Findings

### What was inferable from existing files

- Core concept of the blog was described in `seed.md`.
- Word count constraint (500-1500 words) and standalone essay requirement were explicit in `seed.md`.
- No folder structure existed for essays initially.

### What required human clarification

| Question asked | Why files didn't answer it |
|---|---|
| Folder structure simplification | `seed.md` did not specify how to organize drafts vs published pieces. Human clarified a single flat `essays/` folder with log tracking. |
| Role definitions | Project described a two-role workflow but definitions weren't formal. Human confirmed Owner as Editor and Writer as a distinct role. |
| Workflow details | How work is triggered and how cycles happen. Human confirmed Writer-driven topic selection and a 3-phase revision loop. |

## Patterns Observed
