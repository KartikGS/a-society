## Patterns Observed

- **Root-as-Workspace Pattern**: For projects with a single unit of work in flight (like Ink essays), using the project root as the active "Draft/Review" workspace while moving completed work to a dedicated subfolder (`essays/`) maximizes focus and minimizes "folder-hopping" for agents.
