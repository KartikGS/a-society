# A-Society: Improvement Reports

This folder holds all backward pass findings produced during improvement cycles for the A-Society project.

See `$A_SOCIETY_IMPROVEMENT_PROTOCOL` for the backward pass protocol that governs how these reports are produced.

---

## What Goes Here

| Report Type | Naming Convention | Produced By |
|---|---|---|
| Per-agent findings | `META-YYYYMMDD-<task-id>-<role>-findings.md` | Owner or Curator (backward pass) |

---

## Template

A-Society uses the general template directly:

- `$GENERAL_IMPROVEMENT_TEMPLATE_FINDINGS` — backward pass findings template

> **Note:** A-Society uses the general templates directly rather than copying them here. As the source of the templates, A-Society's general templates are the canonical version.

---

## Conventions

- Report files are **not** edited after creation. They are historical artifacts once produced.
- Do not add non-report files to this folder. Philosophy and protocol documents live at `$A_SOCIETY_IMPROVEMENT` and `$A_SOCIETY_IMPROVEMENT_PROTOCOL`.
