# A-Society: Improvement Reports

This folder holds all improvement reports produced during meta-analysis cycles for the A-Society project.

See `$A_SOCIETY_IMPROVEMENT_PROTOCOL` for the full protocol that governs how these reports are created, structured, and used.

---

## What Goes Here

| Report Type | Naming Convention | Produced By |
|---|---|---|
| Lightweight synthesis | `META-YYYYMMDD-<task-id>-lightweight.md` | Owner (post-task, default) |
| Per-agent findings — Curator | `META-YYYYMMDD-<task-id>-curator-findings.md` | Curator (Phase 1) |
| Per-agent findings — Owner | `META-YYYYMMDD-<task-id>-owner-findings.md` | Owner (Phase 1) |
| Synthesis | `META-YYYYMMDD-<task-id>-synthesis.md` | Owner (Phase 2) |
| Alignment backlog | `ALIGN-YYYYMMDD-backlog.md` | Owner (Mode B) |

---

## Templates

Report templates are sourced directly from A-Society's own general library:

- `$GENERAL_IMPROVEMENT_TEMPLATE_LIGHTWEIGHT` — lightweight synthesis template
- `$GENERAL_IMPROVEMENT_TEMPLATE_FINDINGS` — per-agent findings template
- `$GENERAL_IMPROVEMENT_TEMPLATE_SYNTHESIS` — synthesis template
- `$GENERAL_IMPROVEMENT_TEMPLATE_BACKLOG` — alignment backlog template

> **Note:** A-Society uses the general templates directly rather than copying them here. As the source of the templates, A-Society's general templates are the canonical version.

---

## Conventions

- Report files are **not** edited after creation. They are historical artifacts once produced.
- Synthesis files may be updated with implementation notes after Phase 3 completes (per `$A_SOCIETY_IMPROVEMENT_PROTOCOL`).
- Do not add non-report files to this folder. Philosophy and protocol documents live at `$A_SOCIETY_IMPROVEMENT` and `$A_SOCIETY_IMPROVEMENT_PROTOCOL`.
